# 📈 Promotion Policy

This document defines how information moves from `ORIGIN` (unverified/historical) to `BODY` (canonical/active).

## 1. Intake
New PDFs, links, or chat logs are placed in `BODY/SKELETON/BONES/DOCS/ORIGIN/`.

## 2. Research (EYES)
- A "Source Card" must be created in `BODY/EYES/SOURCES/source_cards/`.
- Facts must be extracted and verified against established `SOURCES`.

## 3. Review (NOSE)
- NOSE must scan the extracted data for risk, drift, or bad smells.

## 4. Incorporation (BODY)
- Once verified, information is "promoted" by updating the relevant organ's documentation.
- The promotion event must be logged in `BODY/HEART/DECISIONS/decision_log.md`.

---
*No information enters the canonical context without following this chain of custody.*
